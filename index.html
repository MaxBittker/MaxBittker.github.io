Hello! I'm a student + programmer + person. I love to tinker at the intersection of code, art and learning.

Hive

A programming game about resource collection and distributed intelligence. Built by myself, Michael Layzell, Erik Pilkington & Jake Pittis in 36 hours for Hack Western. Designed to be a fun challenge for both new and experienced coders

Mojulo

Math visualization sandbox. Renders a grid of pixels based on an equation input by the user. You can use it to explore mathematics, or just enjoy the colors. All creations are easily share-able by copying the URL. Check out the gallery! And tweet your creations at #Mojulo

Propagate

Community aquarium, where colorful pixel flora grow and interact. Each species is defined by a user by writing code a simple lua api. State is persistent, synchronized, and server-side. Currently has an issue where it develops into a monoculture over time...

Forkpad

Built with Pippin Lee at the CODEX Hackathon, this is a tool designed to graft some interactivity and feedback capability into the Wattpad writing + reading community.

Dust

Incarnation of my intense nostalgia for the falling sand genre of games. Light on element choices compared to some of the classics, but as far as I know this is the only HTML5 implementation. Try out the fluid dynamics and the "Glitch" element

Ethical Fishing Simulator

This is a simple local two player game exploring overfishing and the tragedy of the commons. Underneath the social commentary, there's a nice boids-style swarming algorithm
